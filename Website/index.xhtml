~Welcome~

This website was created as part of the Computational Methods in Humanities course at the University of Pittsburgh. The goal of this project was to analyze dissidence in rock music from the Soviet Union. The criteria for analysis can be found under the Criteria tab. The lyrics can be accessed from the Lyrics tab; the methodology for selecting the song sample is also located under the Lyrics tab. The Analysis tab contains the results of our analysis, and the More tab contains references and additional information.
